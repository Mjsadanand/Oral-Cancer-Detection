From upload to medical follow-up in 4 clear steps

This AI assistant is designed to help with fast screening, not a final diagnosis. Use these steps for safer usage.

1

Upload a clear image

Choose a PNG or JPG histopathology image with good clarity and lighting. Blurry or low-quality images reduce confidence.

2

AI performs classification

The model processes the image and predicts a class with confidence level. This gives a quick screening indicator.

3

Review risk and recommendations

Check the predicted label, confidence score, and recommendation list. Use it as supportive information only.

4

Consult an oral specialist

For confirmation and treatment planning, always consult an oral pathologist, oral oncologist, or dentist.

Important Disclaimer

This tool is for screening support only and does not replace professional medical diagnosis, biopsy, or pathology review.
